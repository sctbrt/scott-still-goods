About

Still Goods is an exploration in thoughtful craft and considered use. We're interested in objects that serve quietly, age gracefully, and resist planned obsolescence.

Each piece is selected or made with attention to material, construction, and longevity. We favor natural materials, honest manufacturing, and designs that improve with use.

Approach

We work directly with makers and small manufacturers who share our values. This allows us to understand how things are made and ensure quality at every step.

Our catalog stays intentionally small. Rather than offering everything, we focus on a few well-made essentials that earn their place through daily use.

Values

  • Quality over quantity — Fewer, better things
  • Honest materials — Natural fibers, solid wood, quality metals
  • Considered design — Form that serves function
  • Built to last — Made for repair, not replacement
  • Quiet presence — Objects that don't demand attention

Behind Still Goods

Still Goods is a project by Scott Bertrand, who designs systems and structures that make work feel lighter. This shop extends that philosophy to the physical objects we choose to live with.
